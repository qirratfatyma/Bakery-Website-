About Us

Founded in 1985, PalatePlate started as a home kitchen, blossoming into a beloved bakery. By 1987, their first storefront opened. From bread to gluten-free treats and specialty cakes, their journey is marked by devotion, innovation, and superb flavors. Today, PALATEPLATE is a cherished community institution, celebrated for culinary excellence.
